# Sign in to your space

1. Open the SIGFID Client application
2. Click **Sign in**
3. Enter your email and password

## Forgotten password

Click **Forgotten password** and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link valid for 1 hour.

## Security

- Your session expires after 8 hours of inactivity
- Never share your credentials
- Always sign out after use on a shared computer
