# SIGFID Taxpayer Space

The taxpayer space allows you to:
- View your **tax account** and the status of your obligations
- Track your ongoing **returns**
- Communicate with your **tax centre** via secure messaging

## Access

Your access is created by your tax centre upon your registration. You receive an invitation email to set your password.

## Confidentiality

All your tax data is protected in accordance with the **SIGFID 2026 Confidentiality and Data Protection Protocol**. Only authorised officers of your tax centre can access your file.
