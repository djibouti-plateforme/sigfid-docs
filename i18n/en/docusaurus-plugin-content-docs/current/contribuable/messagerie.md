# Messaging with your tax centre

Messaging allows you to contact your tax centre directly for any question relating to your tax file.

## Start a new conversation

1. Click **Messaging** in the navigation bar
2. Click **New conversation**
3. Enter a subject and your message
4. Click **Send**

An officer of your tax centre will reply as soon as possible.

## Track your conversations

The list of your conversations displays:
- The subject of the conversation
- The date of the last message
- The status (Open / Closed)
- The name of the officer in charge (if assigned)

## Best practice

Always specify in your message:
- Your NIF
- The relevant period
- The tax type
- The precise nature of your question

This enables the officer to process your request more quickly.
