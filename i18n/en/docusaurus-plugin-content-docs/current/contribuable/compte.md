# My tax account

The tax account consolidates all your obligations and payments.

## Information displayed

- **Overall balance**: total of outstanding debts
- **Breakdown by tax**: ITS, VAT, Patente, etc.
- **Payment history**
- **Upcoming deadlines**

## Debt statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Amount due, payment not received |
| **Partial** | Partial payment received |
| **Settled** | Fully paid |

## Download a receipt

For each settled payment, you can download a receipt by clicking the download icon.
