# Sign in to sigfid-console

## Prerequisites

- A functional administrator account created by another administrator
- The URL of the sigfid-console application provided by your IT manager

## Sign-in steps

1. Open sigfid-console in your browser
2. Click **Sign in**
3. Enter your email address and password
4. You are redirected to the dashboard

## First sign-in

On your first sign-in, you receive an invitation email containing a link to set your password. This link is valid for **72 hours**.

:::warning
If the link has expired, ask another administrator to resend an invitation from **Administrators → Detail → Resend invitation**.
:::

## Sign out

Click your name in the top right then **Sign out**. The session expires automatically after 8 hours of inactivity.
