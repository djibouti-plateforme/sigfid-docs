# Manage functional administrators

## Create an administrator

1. In the sidebar, click **Administrators**
2. Click **New administrator**
3. Fill in the form (first name, last name, email)
4. Click **Create**

The administrator receives an invitation email to set their password.

:::info
At least one active administrator must exist in the system at all times. You cannot delete your own account.
:::

## Suspend / Reactivate

Same procedure as for officers. A suspended administrator can no longer access sigfid-console.
