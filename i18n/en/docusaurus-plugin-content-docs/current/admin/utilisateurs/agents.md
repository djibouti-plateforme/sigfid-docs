# Manage tax officers

## Create a new officer

1. In the sidebar, click **Officers**
2. Click **New officer**
3. Fill in the form:
   - First name and last name (required)
   - Email address (required, must be unique)
   - Phone number (optional)
   - Assigned tax centre
4. Click **Create**

The officer automatically receives an invitation email with a link to set their password.

## Officer statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Invitation sent, password not yet set |
| **Active** | Account activated, officer can sign in |
| **Suspended** | Access temporarily blocked |

## Suspend / Reactivate an officer

1. Open the officer's profile
2. In the **Actions** section, click **Suspend** or **Reactivate**

A suspended officer can no longer sign in to sigfid-agent. Their data and history are preserved.

## Transfer an officer to another tax centre

1. Open the officer's profile
2. Click **Transfer**
3. Select the new tax centre
4. Enter the reason for the transfer (optional)
5. Confirm

The transfer history is preserved and viewable in the officer's profile.

## Delete an officer

:::danger
Deletion is irreversible. Prefer suspension if you wish to preserve the history.
:::

1. Open the officer's profile
2. Click **Delete**
3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog box
