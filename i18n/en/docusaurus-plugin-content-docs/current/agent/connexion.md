# Sign in to sigfid-agent

1. Open sigfid-agent in your browser
2. Click **Sign in**
3. Enter your email and password

## First sign-in

You receive an invitation email when your account is created. Click the link to set your password (valid for 72 hours).

## Offline mode

SIGFID works in **offline mode**: if your internet connection is interrupted, you can still browse the data that was already loaded. Modification actions are queued and sent automatically upon reconnection.

An indicator in the application header shows your connection state.
