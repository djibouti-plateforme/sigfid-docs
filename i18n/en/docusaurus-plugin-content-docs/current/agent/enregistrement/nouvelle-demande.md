# Create a registration request

**Required permission:** `ENREGISTREMENT_CREATE`

Registration duties apply to legal acts (contracts, transfers, property changes).  
**CGI reference:** Book 2, Part 1.

## Steps

1. In the sidebar, click **Registration → New request**
2. Select the **type of act** (real estate sale, sale of business assets, lease, etc.)
3. Enter the parties (seller, buyer, or parties to the contract)
4. Enter the **market value** or the **rent amount** depending on the act
5. Attach the required documents (notarised deed, signed lease) via the document service
6. Click **Submit request**

## Required documents

Depending on the type of act:
- Copy of the notarised or private deed
- Title of ownership
- Identity documents of the parties
