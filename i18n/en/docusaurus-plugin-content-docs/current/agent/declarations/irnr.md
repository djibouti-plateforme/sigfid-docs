# Tax on Non-Resident Remuneration (IRNR)

**CGI reference:** Book 1, Title 1, Chapter 5 — Art. 70 to 77  
**Required permission:** `DECLARATION_IRNR_CREATE`  
**Rate:** 25% (LFI 2024) — Withholding at source, definitive

---

## 1. Principle (Art. 70)

IRNR is collected by **withholding at source** on remuneration paid by Djiboutian debtors to beneficiaries **not domiciled in Djibouti**.

**Legally liable party:** the Djiboutian debtor (client), who withholds 25% of the pre-tax gross amount before paying the foreign service provider.

---

## 2. Taxable remuneration (Art. 72)

- Fees, commissions, royalties paid to non-residents
- Remuneration for artistic, sporting, intellectual activities carried out in Djibouti
- Head office expenses billed by the foreign parent company to the Djiboutian branch
- Technical assistance, technology transfer

### Non-taxable remuneration (Art. 72-3°)

- International transport (air, sea, land)
- Purchases of goods abroad

### Exempt remuneration (Art. 73)

- Training provided by approved organisations
- Remuneration covered by international tax treaties
- Aid and technical assistance under government programmes

---

## 3. Base and rate (Art. 74-75)

```
IRNR = Pre-tax gross amount paid × 25%
```

- **Rounding (Art. 75-1°):** fraction < DJF 1,000 disregarded
- **Rate (Art. 75-2°):** 25% (set by LFI 2024)

---

## 4. Enter an IRNR return in SIGFID

1. Click **Returns → IRNR → New return**
2. Select the Djiboutian debtor (the entity making the withholding)
3. Enter for each payment to a non-resident:
   - Identity and country of the beneficiary
   - Nature of the remuneration
   - Pre-tax gross amount
4. SIGFID calculates IRNR = amount × 25%
5. Validate

**Deadline:** before the **15th of the month following** the payment (Art. 76-2°).

---

## 5. Scenarios

### Scenario 1 — Standard service provision
*Djiboutian company pays DJF 2,000,000 pre-tax to a French consultant*

| | Amount (DJF) |
|-|--------------|
| Pre-tax gross amount | 2,000,000 |
| IRNR = 25% × 2,000,000 | **500,000** |
| Paid to the provider | 1,500,000 |

### Scenario 2 — Head office expenses (branch)
*Djiboutian branch pays DJF 5M pre-tax in head office expenses to the French parent*

| | Amount (DJF) |
|-|--------------|
| Pre-tax gross head office expenses | 5,000,000 |
| IRNR = 25% × 5M | **1,250,000** |

### Scenario 3 — Exempt transaction (training)
*Approved training organisation, amount = DJF 3,000,000 pre-tax*

| | Amount (DJF) |
|-|--------------|
| Training amount | 3,000,000 |
| Applicable IRNR | **0** (exempt Art. 73) |

---

## 6. Penalties (Art. 77 → Art. 249)

| Infringement | Penalty | Article |
|--------------|---------|---------|
| Failure to withhold | **100% of the non-withheld amount** | Art. 249 |
| Late payment | 0.5%/month + 5% | Art. 247 |
