# Messaging with taxpayers

**Required permission:** `REPONDRE_MESSAGERIE`

Messaging allows officers to communicate directly with taxpayers via the SIGFID platform.

## View conversations

1. In the sidebar, click **Messaging**
2. The list displays all conversations of your tax centre

Each conversation shows:
- The subject
- The taxpayer
- The date of the last message
- The status (Open / Closed)

## Reply to a message

1. Open the conversation
2. Enter your reply in the text area
3. Click **Send**

The taxpayer receives a notification and can view your reply in their space.

## Close a conversation

When the taxpayer's question has been resolved, click **Close conversation**. The taxpayer can still view the history but can no longer send new messages in this conversation.
