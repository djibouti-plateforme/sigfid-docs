<section className="glossaire-letter" id="A">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">A</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="doc">AMR</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Notice of Tax Collection (Avis de Mise en Recouvrement)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Administrative act by which the DGI notifies the taxpayer of the amount of taxes, penalties and late interest to be paid. Constitutes an enforceable title.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Tax base</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Calculation basis for the tax (taxable income, turnover, value added, etc.). Determining the tax base is the step preceding assessment.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Taxable person</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Natural or legal person legally bound to declare and pay a tax. Depending on the tax, liability may be by operation of law, by option, or conditional on a threshold.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Audit trail</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Immutable log of all actions performed in SIGFID: who did what, when, on which file. Used as administrative and legal evidence.</p>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="B">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">B</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Progressive bracket scale</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Table defining income brackets and the marginal rate applicable to each. Used for the ITS (8 brackets, from 0% to 39%).</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 15 CGI</span>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="concept">DB</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Database</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Central repository where SIGFID's taxpayers, returns, payments and tax parameters are stored. Single source of truth.</p>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="C">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">C</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="concept">CA</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Turnover (Chiffre d'affaires)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Total sales or services of a taxable person over a given period. Used as the tax base for several taxes (IMF, CP) and as a VAT liability criterion.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Tax centre</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Territorial office of the DGI in charge of monitoring a portfolio of taxpayers. Configured in SIGFID by the functional administrator.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="doc">CGI</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">General Tax Code (Code Général des Impôts)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Legislative compilation gathering all tax provisions applicable in the Republic of Djibouti. Ultimate reference of the SIGFID documentation.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">CL</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Licence Contribution (Contribution des Licences)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Annual tax due by operators of licensed activities (alcohol, gambling, etc.).</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 130-136 CGI</span>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">CP</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Patente Contribution (Contribution des Patentes)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Annual tax due by any person carrying on a commercial, industrial or professional activity.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 91-129 CGI</span>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="D">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">D</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Nil return</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Return mandatorily filed even in the absence of taxable transactions for the period. Prevents the non-filer qualification and automatic penalties.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="org">DGI</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">General Directorate of Taxes (Direction Générale des Impôts)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Tax administration of the Republic of Djibouti, issuing authority of the SIGFID documentation and owner of the system.</p>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="E">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">E</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Deadline</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Date by which a return must be filed or a tax paid. SIGFID automatically applies penalties from the day after the deadline.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Exemption</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Legal dispensation from paying a tax. For VAT, take care to distinguish between exemption (no right of deduction) and zero rate (right of deduction preserved).</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 176 CGI (VAT)</span>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="H">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">H</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Historisation</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Immutable preservation of the state of a piece of data at the time of an event. SIGFID stores every tax calculation with a snapshot of the parameters used.</p>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="I">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">I</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">IBP</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Tax on Business Profits (Impôt sur les Bénéfices Professionnels)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Tax on the profits of companies and individual entrepreneurs. Rate: 25% of taxable profits.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Book 1, Title 1, Ch. 2</span>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">IIS</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Social Incentive Tax (Impôt d'Incitation Sociale)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Tax on wages dedicated to funding social protection.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Book 1, Title 1, Ch. 6</span>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">IMF</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Minimum Lump-Sum Tax (Impôt Minimum Forfaitaire)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Minimum tax due by companies, calculated at 1% of pre-tax turnover (floor DJF 50,000, ceiling DJF 5,000,000).</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Book 1, Title 1, Ch. 3</span>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">IPVI</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Real Estate Capital Gains Tax (Impôt sur la Plus-Value Immobilière)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Tax on capital gains realised upon the sale of a real estate asset.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">IPVM</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Securities Capital Gains Tax (Impôt sur la Plus-Value Mobilière)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Tax on capital gains realised upon the sale of securities.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">IRNR</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Tax on Non-Resident Remuneration (Impôt sur les Rémunérations Non-Résidents)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Withholding tax on remuneration paid to recipients not domiciled in Djibouti.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Book 1, Title 1, Ch. 5</span>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">IRVM</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Tax on Income from Securities (Impôt sur le Revenu des Valeurs Mobilières)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Tax on dividends, interest and similar income from securities.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">ITS</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Wages and Salaries Tax (Impôt sur les Traitements et Salaires)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Tax on wage income, withheld at source by the employer according to a progressive 8-bracket scale.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 1-17 CGI</span>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="L">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">L</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="doc">LFI</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Initial Finance Act (Loi de Finances Initiale)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Annual law adopted by Parliament setting the State's revenues and expenditures. May amend rates, thresholds or create new taxes.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Assessment (Liquidation)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Actual calculation of the amount of tax due from the base and applicable parameters. Carried out automatically by SIGFID from the return.</p>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="M">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">M</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Surcharge (Majoration)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Increase of the amount payable in case of infringement or delay. For VAT, 10% surcharge beyond one month of delay.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 246-250 CGI</span>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="N">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">N</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="concept">NIF</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Tax Identification Number (Numéro d'Identification Fiscale)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Unique identifier assigned to each taxpayer by the DGI. Mandatory on all VAT invoices to give rise to the right of deduction.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 208-210 CGI</span>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="P">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">P</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Penalty</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Pecuniary sanction applied in case of filing default, late payment, or infringement. Calculated and applied automatically by SIGFID.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 246-265 CGI</span>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Deduction prorata</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Coefficient (taxable turnover / total turnover) applied to determine the share of VAT deductible by a taxable person simultaneously carrying out taxable and exempt activities.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 192 CGI</span>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="R">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">R</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="concept">RAS</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Withholding at Source (Retenue à la Source)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Mechanism by which the payer (employer, client) withholds the tax on the income paid and remits it directly to the Treasury.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">RAS PS</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Withholding on Services (Retenue à la Source sur Prestations de Service)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Withholding applicable to service payments made by certain taxpayers.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 298 CGI</span>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Collection</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">All operations enabling the administration to collect amounts owed by taxpayers (formal notice, AMR, enforcement procedures).</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Reassessment</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Correction by the tax administration of the amount of tax declared by the taxpayer, following an audit.</p>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="S">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">S</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="org">SIGFID</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Integrated Tax Management System of Djibouti</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Digital platform of the DGI, subject of this documentation. Comprises three applications: <strong>sigfid-console</strong> (admin), <strong>sigfid-agent</strong> (officers) and the taxpayer space.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Tax snapshot</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Immutable capture of the parameters (rates, brackets, thresholds) used at the time of a tax calculation. Guarantees the stability of past assessments even after subsequent parameter changes.</p>
</div>

  </div>
</section>

<section className="glossaire-letter" id="T">
  <div className="glossaire-letter-mark">T</div>
  <div className="glossaire-entries">

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">TBA</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Air Ticket Tax (Taxe sur les Billets d'Avion)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Tax payable by airlines on tickets issued for departures from Djibouti.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Book 1, Title 1, Ch. 8</span>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-name">Bracket</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Segment of a progressive scale delimited by a minimum and maximum income, to which a given marginal rate applies.</p>
</div>

<div className="glossaire-entry">
<div className="glossaire-term">
<span className="glossaire-acronym" data-kind="impot">VAT</span>
<span className="glossaire-name">Value Added Tax (Taxe sur la Valeur Ajoutée — TVA)</span>
</div>
<p className="glossaire-def">Indirect tax on consumption, ultimately borne by the end consumer. Standard rate: 10%. Zero rate for exports.</p>
<span className="glossaire-ref">Art. 171-211 CGI</span>
</div>

  </div>
</section>
